AMEC2002 Abstracts
 
"EXPLORING THE RED PLANET"

In anticipation of the publication of the proceedings of the 2nd Annual Australian Mars Exploration Conference, abstracts of the papers presented at the conference have been released for public viewing. Feel free to download and read these documents by clicking on the title of the each paper. Each abstract is in PDF format and to view these file you may need to download the free program Adobe Acrobat Reader 5.0. Each file is ~100Kb and cannot be republished without the express permission of Mars Society Australia and the AMEC2002 Organising Committee.

 
If you wish to purchase a copy of the AMEC2002 Conference Proceedings when they become available please contact Michael West
